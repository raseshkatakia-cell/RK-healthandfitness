WORKOUTS
BEGINNER
Full-body beginner routine
Low impact — perfect for starting out. Do this 3× per week.
Bodyweight Squats
3 sets × 12 reps — Keep chest up, sit back into hips, knees tracking toes.
Incline Push-ups
3 sets × 8–12 reps — Use a bench or wall if needed. Keep core tight.
Plank
3 × 30–45 seconds — Keep a straight line from head to heels, breathe steadily.
Tip: warm up 5–8 minutes before starting (light cardio + mobility).
INTERMEDIATE
Split routine — strength & conditioning
4 sessions per week: Push / Pull / Legs / Core+Cardio
Bent-over Dumbbell Rows
4 sets × 8–10 reps — Use controlled tempo, squeeze shoulder blades.
Walking Lunges
3 sets × 12 steps per leg — Keep torso upright, step long enough to load glute.
Mountain Climbers
4 × 40 seconds — High tempo to raise heart rate & core engagement.
Tip: progressive overload — increase weight or reps slightly every week.
ADVANCED
High intensity — strength & fat loss
Push your limits. 5–6 sessions/week with heavy lifts & conditioning.
Barbell Deadlifts
5 sets × 5 reps — Heavy, focus on form: neutral spine, drive hips forward.
Kettlebell Swings
4 sets × 15 reps — Hinge, explosive hips, controlled return.
Sprint Intervals
10 × 30s sprint / 60s walk — Max effort for fat loss & conditioning.
Warning: advanced sessions require good recovery and nutrition. Consult a professional if unsure.
